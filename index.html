SquishLeft

Fits any image into the left 675×1080 region of a 1920×1080 black frame and exports a JPG. Everything runs in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

Drop any image here

or click to choose a file · PNG, JPG, WEBP, etc.

Output preview (1920×1080)

What it does:
  • Stretches any image to exactly 675×1080.
  • Places it on the left of a 1920×1080 black canvas.
  • Exports a 1920×1080 JPG at 95% quality.
